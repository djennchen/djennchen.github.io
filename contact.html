About me

Hi! My name is Jenny. I grew up in San Diego and have spent time in Philadelphia and San Francisco as well. Currently, I've been working remotely, which has given me the chance to be a bit more of a wanderer.

That being said, I'm 100% not a spontaneous traveler. I love to plan and rely on the internet (and travel blogs!) to inform my journeys. One of my 2022 New Year's Resolutions was to start up my own travel blog to chronicle my process and share my memories and itineraries.

I hope you find this blog useful, inspirational, or just fun to read! It is most definitely still a work in progress :) Feel free to contact me at djennchen [at] gmail.com.
